Research Experience

  • Advanced Machine Learning Applications in Dynamo Lab (March 2025 – Present)

    At the University of Virginia’s Department of Computer Science, I am currently working with Heman Shakeri in the Dynamo Lab on advanced machine learning applications aimed at addressing complex data analysis challenges.

  • Foundation Model for Astronomy in Infomall Lab (March 2023 – Feb 2025)

    I collaborated with Prof. Geoffrey Fox in the Infomall Lab on developing foundation models for astrophysics data, which provided a strong background in advanced deep learning techniques.

  • Swarm Intelligence Optimizer for the LIME Algorithm (April 2022 – Dec 2023)

    At the University of Windsor, I enhanced the Local Interpretable Model-agnostic Explanations (LIME) algorithm by integrating a swarm intelligence optimizer. This project improved the algorithm’s efficiency and interpretability, drawing inspiration from natural collective behavior.

  • Medical Data Preprocessing and Modeling (Feb 2022 – April 2022)

    At Shiraz University of Technology’s Data Modeling, Processing, and Analysis Research Group, I focused on cleaning, analyzing, and modeling medical datasets to extract meaningful features and enhance predictive models.

  • Enhanced Bat Algorithm for Hyperparameter Tuning (June 2020 – April 2022)

    At the Electrical and Electronic Engineering Department of Shiraz University of Technology, I refined the bat algorithm—a metaheuristic optimization technique—and applied it to hyperparameter tuning in deep neural networks to optimize training processes and improve model performance.
