Amirreza Dolatpour Fathkouhi

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Virginia, advised by Prof. Heman Shakeri[cite: 4, 6, 7]. My research is currently based at the Center for Diabetes Technology, focusing on deep learning applications for healthcare.

My work centers on developing robust imputation methodologies and counterfactual inference for complex, dynamic Type 1 Diabetes (T1D) datasets, as well as exploring foundation models for time-series and scientific data[cite: 12, 25, 29].
